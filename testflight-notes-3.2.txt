Casalist 1 (3.2)

What's New
- Family roles: Owner / Admin (max 2) / Member
- Cottage-themed Settings tab with role badges, role management, and ownership transfer
- Admins and Owners can adjust any family member's points (± 5) from the Chore Rewards leaderboard

What's Fixed
- Schema mismatch causing "newer version of Casalist is needed" — CloudKit Production schema now matches the binary (added CD_roleLevel field on CD_FamilyMember)

What to Test
- Open the app — no schema error
- Open Settings: tap a member's role badge to promote/demote them (only owner sees this)
- Open Chore Rewards as an admin or owner: tap the ± buttons next to each member's points
- Tap your own crown to transfer ownership to another member
