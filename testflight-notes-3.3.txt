Casalist 1 (3.3)

What's Fixed
- iCloud share invites: added CKSharingSupported = YES to Info.plist. Without that key, iOS shows "you need a newer version" when a recipient taps a Casalist share link, because it doesn't know the app handles CloudKit share URLs.

What to Test
- From the owner's phone, open Invite Family and send a share link
- On the recipient's phone (also on 3.3), tap the link
- Casalist should open and accept the share
