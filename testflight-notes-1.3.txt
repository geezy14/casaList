Casalist 1 (build 1.3)

This is the first family build. Here's what's inside.

CORE
- Family chores, goals, rewards, leaderboard. Points roll up per
 member.
- Kid mode: family members tagged as Kid get a dedicated big-tap UI
 with chores, goals, and a wins log. Set the role in Settings.
- Three color themes (Ember default, Vivid, Anchor) in
 Settings -> Appearance. Swap anytime.
- Pull-to-refresh on every screen when sync feels slow.

REWARDS
- Anyone can ask for a reward with an optional "why." Admins set the
 points price at approval.
- Pending requests show as a peach badge on the requester's avatar
 in the standings row. Tap to approve inline.

NOTIFICATIONS
- Pushes when a chore is assigned to you, when a kid asks for a
 reward, when someone redeems, and a weekly recap on Sundays.

REMINDERS
- Daily / weekly / monthly / yearly reminders grow their own streak
 counter. Miss a cycle, it resets, but the best stays.
- Hourly reminders can take an optional stop time so they don't buzz
 through the night.

ADMIN
- Settings -> Data -> "Reset all data" wipes chores, goals, points,
 streaks, and badges, but keeps the household and every member.

WHAT TO TEST
- Add a chore for someone else and watch them get a push within ~30s.
- Have a kid hit "Ask for a reward." Approve from their standings
 avatar with a points price.
- Make a daily reminder and check it off two days in a row to see
 the streak build.
