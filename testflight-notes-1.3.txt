Casalist 1 (build 1.3)

REMINDERS GET BETTER
- Each repeating reminder now grows its own streak counter. Check it
 off on cadence (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly) and watch the
 number climb. Miss a cycle and it resets, but the all-time best
 is preserved.
- Hourly and every-N-hour reminders gain an optional STOP TIME. Set
 "Drink water every hour from 8am to 10pm" and it stops firing at
 night instead of buzzing through every hour around the clock.

WHAT TO TEST
- Create a daily reminder. Check it off today and tomorrow. The card
 should show a 2-day streak. Skip a day and watch it reset (but the
 best count sticks).
- Create an hourly reminder. Turn on Stop time and pick something
 like 9pm. Verify notifications only fire between the start hour
 and 9pm.
