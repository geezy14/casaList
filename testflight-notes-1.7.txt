Casalist 1.7 — Reminder rebuild + Apple Reminders link

The Reminders surface got a complete rebuild this release. The biggest changes:

Apple Reminders link
- Settings → REMINDERS lets you pick an Apple Reminders list. Casalist reminders mirror into that list automatically.
- That list's non-Casalist items show up at the bottom of the Reminders screen in a "FROM YOUR APPLE REMINDERS" section (read-only).

New reminder authoring sheet
- Apple Reminders style: title field, row of chip buttons (When / Repeats / Notify / Location / Photo / Tag / Sound / Stop time), tap a chip to expand its picker inline.
- When you open an existing reminder to edit, every populated chip is auto-expanded so you see everything at once.

Unified Repeat picker
- One sheet for everything. Don't repeat / quick-pick chips (Hourly, Daily, Weekly, etc.) / custom builder for "every 2 weeks on Friday" style cadences.

Lock-screen actions
- Long-press a reminder push: Mark done / Snooze 15 min / Snooze 1 hour / Snooze until tomorrow.

Per-family-member reminders
- "Notify" picker lets you pick which family member's phone fires the reminder. Default is everyone.

Location-based reminders
- "Trigger at a location" toggle in the reminder sheet. Pick a place, choose Arriving or Leaving, set the radius (100 ft to 1 mile). Mini-map shows the geofence.
- Settings → SAVED LOCATIONS lets you save Home / Work / School once and use them as quick-pick chips.

Photo attachments
- Camera chip on the reminder sheet attaches a photo from your library. Shows as a thumbnail on the pinned card. Device-local — photos do not sync to other family members.

Reminder history
- Clock icon on the Reminders top bar opens a "what fired / what I marked done / what I snoozed" log, sectioned by Today / Yesterday / This week / Older.

Templates
- "Save as template" button in the reminder sheet saves the cadence + assignee + location + stop time as a reusable starting point. Stacked-squares icon on the Reminders top bar opens the template picker.

Color tags
- Tag chip in the reminder sheet picks one of 7 colors. Shows as a stripe on the left edge of the pinned card.

Drag-to-reorder
- Long-press a pinned reminder for context menu options: Pin to top / Send to bottom / Duplicate.

Streak heatmap
- For daily/weekly/monthly/yearly reminders, the edit sheet shows a 30-day heatmap of completions, plus current and best streak.

Daily recap
- Settings → Notifications → "Daily reminder recap" fires one summary push at your chosen hour (default 9 PM): X done, Y fired, Z snoozed today.

Sound on/off per reminder
- Speaker chip in the reminder sheet flips between sound-on (default) and silent for that specific reminder.

Notification grouping
- Multiple reminder pushes firing close together get threaded in Notification Center.

Title autocomplete
- Start typing a reminder title, see recent matching titles as chips. Tap to fill.

Other fixes and polish
- Household name now syncs across devices. Setting it in Settings used to only save locally; now writes to the shared Household record.
- Mute a family member's pushes: bell icon in Settings → FAMILY, durations of 1 hour / 4 hours / until tomorrow. Per-device. Honors status pings + grocery activity.
- Location radius now displayed in feet and miles (US units).

What to test
- Create a reminder with a location trigger, leave the geofence area, confirm the notification fires.
- Mark a reminder done from the lock screen. Confirm it advances to the next occurrence for recurring reminders.
- Snooze a reminder for 15 minutes from the lock screen. Confirm the follow-up fires.
- Assign a reminder to a specific family member. Confirm only that person's phone fires the notification.
- Pick an Apple Reminders list in Settings. Create a reminder in Casalist. Confirm it appears in iOS Reminders.app in that list.
- Set the household name in Settings on one device. Confirm it appears on the other family member's device within 30 seconds.
- Save a reminder as a template. Use the templates picker to create a new reminder from it. Confirm everything carries over.
- Attach a photo to a reminder. Confirm the thumbnail shows on the pinned card.

Known gotchas
- Photo attachments are device-local. They do not sync to other family members. Each device keeps its own reminder photos.
- Apple Reminders mirroring is one-way (Casalist to Apple). Completing a reminder in iOS Reminders.app does not yet mark it done in Casalist. Two-way sync coming in v2.
- Skip-next-occurrence is not yet available. Mark done or Snooze are the lock-screen options for now. The skip action requires a deeper notification-scheduling rework, planned for v2.
