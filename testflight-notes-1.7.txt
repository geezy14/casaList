Casalist 1.7 -- Reminder rebuild + Apple Reminders link

What's new

Apple Reminders link
- Settings -> REMINDERS picks an Apple Reminders list. Casalist reminders mirror there automatically. That list's non-Casalist items appear in Casalist's Reminders screen (read-only).

New authoring sheet for reminders
- Apple Reminders style: title field, row of chip buttons (When / Repeats / Notify / Location / Photo / Tag / Sound / Stop time), tap a chip to expand its picker inline. Editing auto-expands every populated chip.

Unified Repeat picker
- One sheet for everything: Don't repeat / quick-pick chips (Hourly, Daily, Weekly, etc.) / builder for custom cadences like "every 2 weeks on Friday".

Lock-screen actions
- Long-press a reminder push: Mark done / Snooze 15 min / Snooze 1 hour / Snooze until tomorrow.

Per-family-member reminders
- "Notify" picker assigns a reminder to one family member; only their phone fires.

Location-based reminders
- "Trigger at a location" toggle. Pick a place, Arriving or Leaving, radius 100 ft to 1 mile. Settings -> SAVED LOCATIONS saves Home / Work / School once for quick-pick.

Photo attachments
- Camera chip attaches a photo from your library. Thumbnail shows on the pinned card. Device-local -- doesn't sync to other family members.

Reminder history
- Clock icon on the Reminders top bar opens a sectioned log of what fired, what you marked done, what you snoozed.

Templates
- "Save as template" persists cadence + assignee + location + stop time. Stacked-squares icon opens the picker.

Color tags
- Tag chip picks one of 7 colors. Shows as a stripe on the left edge of the pinned card.

Drag-to-reorder + Duplicate
- Long-press a pinned reminder for Pin to top / Send to bottom / Duplicate.

Streak heatmap
- Daily/weekly/monthly/yearly reminders show a 30-day completion heatmap in the edit sheet.

Daily recap
- Settings -> Notifications -> "Daily reminder recap" fires one push at your chosen hour (default 9 PM): X done, Y fired, Z snoozed today.

Sound on/off per reminder + notification grouping
- Speaker chip flips sound on or off per reminder. Multiple reminder pushes firing close together thread in Notification Center.

Title autocomplete
- Type a prefix, see recent matching titles as chips. Tap to fill.

Other fixes and polish
- Household name now syncs across devices (was a phantom local-only edit before).
- Mute a family member's pushes from Settings -> FAMILY for 1 hour / 4 hours / until tomorrow.
- Location radius shown in feet and miles (US units).
