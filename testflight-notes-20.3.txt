What's Fixed in 3 (20.3)

FAMILY LIST POLISH
- Removed the duplicate task-creator + on the Family List top bar. The coral + on this tab now opens the family outing creator only. Chores still live on My To-Do.

FAMILY OUTINGS CAN SPAN MULTIPLE DAYS
- New outings now have separate Starts and Ends date pickers, so weekend trips and longer outings can be scheduled cleanly. The optional time toggle still works the same way.
- The paired Schedule event respects the span. A multi-day all-day outing now shows the full date range; single-day outings behave exactly as before.
