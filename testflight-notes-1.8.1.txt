What's New in 1.8.1

Bug Fix: Swipe-back gesture now works on all screens in the app. Previously the left-edge swipe to go back did not fire reliably (it lost the gesture race to scroll views). Rebuilt with a UIKit gesture recognizer that runs alongside scroll views instead of competing with them.

What to Test

- Open any reminder or task detail screen
- Swipe from the left edge of the screen to go back
- Should dismiss the screen smoothly, including when the page is scrollable
