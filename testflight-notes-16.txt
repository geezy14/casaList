What's New in 3 (16)

IMPORTANT: EVERYONE UPDATE TOGETHER
- Please have every family member update to this build. It does a full reset, and older builds can interfere if they're still open.

FULL FRESH START
- Every member resets to 0 points and back to Rookie (level 1). Admins re-grant points from a clean slate.
- This is a one-time reset on this update.

ADMIN CAN NOW SUBTRACT POINTS
- The minus button on the leaderboard now actually lowers a member's score (it used to only affect the spendable balance). Plus and minus both work now.

LEVELS CHART
- Settings -> Game Rules -> LEVELS shows every level, its name, tier, total points needed, and how many more points to reach it. Levels come fast early, then take more.

EVENT NOTIFICATIONS
- When making a calendar event, the Who section lets you choose who gets notified: Whole household, Admins only, or Just the attendee.

What to Test
- Confirm everyone is at 0 points / Rookie after updating.
- As an admin, use + and - on the leaderboard and confirm both move the score.
- Check Settings -> Game Rules -> LEVELS for the thresholds.
- Confirm chore and reminder notifications still work as before.
