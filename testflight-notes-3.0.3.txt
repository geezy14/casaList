Casalist 3.0 (build 3)

What's new
- Weekly calendar events now sync to Apple Calendar correctly.
  A "weekly every Monday" event repeats every Monday, not just
  the first week. Daily / weekdays-only / monthly / yearly and
  custom intervals all carry across now too.
- Calendar mirror also honors your event end time and applies
  rule changes to the whole series when you edit.
- New Inbox on the tray icon. Three shapes in a picker:
  Activity (live feed of completions, redemptions, approvals),
  Standings (this week's race + leader card), Goals (in-flight
  progress bars). Switch any time.

What to test
- Schedule a weekly recurring event with a kid as the attendee.
  Confirm it shows up on every following Monday in Apple Calendar.
- Tap the tray icon on Dashboard / My To-Do / Family list.
  Cycle through the three Inbox shapes.
- Confirm cross-device sync is still instant.
