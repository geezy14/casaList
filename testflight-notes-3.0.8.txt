Casalist 3.0 (build 8)

Bug fixes.

What's fixed
- Recurring quests clear correctly. Checking off a repeating chore
  now drops it from ACTIVE QUESTS until its next scheduled day,
  including chores with no due date and weekly / weekday / "every
  N" cadences (not just the simple daily/weekly presets).
- Repeat picker opens reliably. Tapping the Repeat field no longer
  flashes open and closes instantly on the first tap (chores,
  reminders, and events).
- Haptics hold up. Checking off several chores in a row no longer
  silences the buzz after a few taps.

What to test
- Mark a weekday (Mon-Fri) chore done. Confirm it leaves ACTIVE
  QUESTS and comes back on the next scheduled day.
- Tap the Repeat field on a new chore / reminder / event and
  confirm the picker stays open on the first tap.
- Check off several chores quickly and confirm the haptic keeps
  firing.
