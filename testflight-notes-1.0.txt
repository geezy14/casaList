Casalist 1.0 (build 1.0) — What's new

The big one: kids can now have their own space.

KID MODE (NEW)
• Family members marked as Kid get a full-screen, simplified view designed
  for them — big tap targets, a clear list of "MY STUFF TO DO", a giant
  points number, a goal shelf showing what they're saving toward, a peek
  at what the rest of the family is working on, and a "MY WINS" trophy
  log of recent chore completions + redeemed rewards.
• "Ask for a reward" — kids can suggest what they want. A parent decides
  if yes and sets the price at approval time.
• Confetti + haptic feedback when a chore is checked off.

THEMES (NEW)
• Settings → Appearance now has a color palette picker. Three themes:
  Ember (warm coral, default), Vivid (saturated jewel tones), and Anchor
  (cobalt + carrot orange).

ACTIVITY FEED (UPGRADED)
• WHAT'S NEW on the dashboard now shows completions and reward
  redemptions across the family, sorted by when they actually happened
  (not when the task was created). Format: "Alex completed Make bed · 2m"
  or "Alex added Trash to Riley · 5m" so it's clear who did what for whom.

NOTIFICATIONS (NEW)
• "You got a new task" push when someone assigns a chore to you.
• "Reward requested" push to parents/admins when a kid suggests a reward.
• "Reward redeemed" push when a family member redeems a goal.
• Weekly Sunday-evening recap with standings.

GOAL REDESIGN
• Anyone proposing a goal (non-admin) writes a label + optional "why".
  Admins set the points price at approval time. Cleaner economy.
• Goal shelf instead of one-at-a-time: see up to 5 active goals, each
  with progress.

DASHBOARD POLISH
• "Home" tile replaces "Maintenance" — bundles home + maintenance chores.
  A pill toggle inside switches between the two categories.
• Quick-add chips on Home for one-tap chore creation. Long-press a chip
  to remove it; menu to clear all.
• "Schedule" tile now uses sky blue, "Family List" uses warm orange —
  no two tiles share a color.
• My To-Do has a Mine/Everyone scope toggle for admins so parents can
  see all family chores in one place.
• Pull-to-refresh on every scrollable screen for snappier sync feel.
• App is back to 2 swipe pages (Home and Rewards). My To-Do and Family
  List still open from their dashboard tiles.

UNDER THE HOOD
• New CloudKit schema fields for completion timestamps, reward request
  notes, and routine templates — deployed to Production.
• Faster onscreen palette switching.

WHAT TO TEST
• Kid mode: set a family member's role to Kid and verify their phone
  switches to the kid view. Add chores assigned to them; check off;
  watch the confetti, the win, the points bump.
• Reward requests: as a kid, ask for a reward with a note. As parent,
  approve with a price.
• Push notifications: assign a chore to another family member on a
  different iCloud account; they should get a banner within a minute.
• Themes: swap palettes in Settings and confirm the entire app re-skins
  instantly.
