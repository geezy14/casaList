Version 2.0 -- Reminders redesign

What's New:

- Reminders sheet completely rebuilt in Apple Reminders style. Date & Time,
  Repeat, Details, and Location are always visible as grouped cards -- no
  icon strip to tap through. Set a date without a time (fires at 9 AM) or
  flip the Time toggle to pick a specific hour.

- Priority on reminders. Set None / Low / Medium / High from the Details
  card. Cards show a colored badge (! / !! / !!!) in the header.

- Task due dates now support date-only mode too. Toggle Time off to mark
  something due today without locking to a specific time.

- Reminder cards have rounder corners.

- Swipe-back gesture works reliably on iOS 26 (hotfix from 1.8.1 carried
  forward).

What to Test:

- Create a reminder with Date on, Time off -- confirm it fires at 9 AM
- Set priority High on a reminder -- confirm !!! badge appears on the card
- Edit an existing reminder -- confirm all panels show populated values
- Add a task due "today" with no time set -- confirm it saves cleanly
