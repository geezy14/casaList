Version 2.0 -- Build 1

What's New:

Starfield (kid mode) overhaul. Four themes: Space, Ocean, Garden, Orchid --
kids pick their own in the gear menu. Two display modes: Simple (big buttons,
just the chores) and Full (stats, family peek, wins). Completing a chore now
animates the checkmark. Tapping your avatar opens your full stats card.

Personal stats card is now available to everyone. Tap your profile photo on
the Home tab to see your points, streak, completions, and year-end projection.

Reminders got a full redesign. Date & Time, Repeat, Details, and Location now
show as grouped cards -- always visible, nothing hidden behind an icon strip.

Priority on reminders. Set None / Low / Medium / High from the Details card.
Cards show a colored badge (! / !! / !!!) in the header.
