Casalist 1 (3.8)

What's Fixed
- Sharing failures when the recipient had been holding orphan records from older app versions ("Could not prepare share: Cocoa error 134410"). The inviter's app now adopts any orphan FamilyMember/TaskItem/etc. records into the household before sharing, ensures a household exists, and retries the share up to 6 times while CloudKit catches up exporting the just-created household record.
- The "belongs to 2 households" reconciliation from 3.6 still applies on the recipient side.

What to Test

- Once accepted, recipient should see exactly one household — the shared one.
