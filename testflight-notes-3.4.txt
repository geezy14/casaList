Casalist 1 (3.4)

What's Fixed
- Share invites: CKSharingSupported = YES is now actually in the binary. Previous 3.3 build had the project setting but Xcode's INFOPLIST_KEY_* mechanism silently dropped it. Switched to an explicit Info.plist so the key takes effect.
