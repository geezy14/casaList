Casalist 3.0 (build 5)

What's new
- Curated redeemable items. Rewards tab has a new REDEEM section
  with 10 pre-built rewards (screen time, gaming, pick the movie,
  pick dinner, stay up late, skip a chore, ice cream trip, store
  trip, arcade day, small toy). Tap one to send a request to an
  admin at a fixed point cost.
- Three layouts for the catalog: Grid, List, or Sheet. Switch
  any time with the segmented picker.
- Items you've already requested show a PENDING pill so you
  don't double-submit.
- Items you can't afford yet show "Need X more pts" so kids
  see what they're working toward.
- Admins can customize the catalog. Settings -> Game Rules ->
  REDEEMABLE ITEMS lets you add, edit, or delete any item.
