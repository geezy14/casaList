Casalist 1 (3.9) — first TestFlight build 🏡

What is this?
Casalist is a private family household app. Two adults + two kids in one household, sharing chores, points, goals, schedule, and a shared family list. Everything syncs across iCloud accounts via Apple's CloudKit.

How to get started
1. Open the app. Type your name when prompted — that's how the family knows who you are.
2. If you're the household creator: tap the person+badge icon (top-right of the dashboard) and share the invite with the other adults' / kids' iCloud accounts via iMessage.
3. If you got an invite: open the iMessage, tap the share link, then come back and finish the name prompt. You'll appear in the family list within ~30s.

Who's who
- Owner: the household creator. Crown badge. Can do everything.
- Admin: a trusted adult. Can invite, manage chores, adjust points.
- Standard: regular adult. Can claim, complete, and propose goals.
- Kid: simplified view; can submit goals for parent approval, complete their own chores, redeem rewards.

What's in this build
- 4 swipe tabs: Home dashboard / My To-Do / Family List / Family Leaderboard.
- Around the House tiles: Grocery, Maintenance, My To-Do, Reminders, Schedule, Family List — tap any to dive in.
- Schedule events with **map-backed locations** — pick a place, see a mini-map.
- Family List ("the family wall") — drop a chore anyone can Claim or Mark Done.
- Approval inbox (tray icon) — kids submit goals; parents approve or deny.
- Quick-add chips on Home — re-spawn recent chores with one tap (parent-only).
- Trash (Settings → Data → Trash) — deleted things linger 30 days; tap Restore.
- iCloud Drive auto-backup (Settings → Backup) — daily JSON snapshot of everything.
- Profile photos sync across devices (you can finally see Mom's face on Dad's phone).

What to poke / report
- Onboarding: does the share-link → name prompt → "I see them on the leaderboard" flow work cleanly?
- Two-account sync: does completing a chore on one phone update points on the other phone within ~30s?
- Photos: set a profile photo on one device — does it appear on the other?
- Goal approval: as a kid, submit a goal → as a parent, check the inbox (tray icon, top-right) → Approve or Deny.
- Location picker: add a Schedule event → tap "Pick a location" → search → confirm the mini-map shows the pin.
- Trash: delete a task or member → Settings → Data → Trash → Restore.
- Backup: Settings → Backup → "Back up now" → check Files app → iCloud Drive → Casalist → Backups for the .casabackup file.

Known limitations
- Older redeemed denials in the inbox don't persist — only currently-pending submissions are shown.
- If you flip a member's role from Kid back to Standard, the device they're on may need a foreground/relaunch to update its UI.
- The "Restore" backup is additive — it imports anything missing but doesn't overwrite existing records. If you want a clean rewind, use Trash for individual records.
