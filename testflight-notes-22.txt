What's New in 3 (22) - the supercharge build

SIRI, SHORTCUTS + SPOTLIGHT
- Casalist now works hands-free. Try: "Add a chore to Casalist", "Mark a chore done in Casalist", "Plan a family outing in Casalist", "Redeem a reward in Casalist."
- All four actions also appear in the Shortcuts app and Spotlight search, so you can automate them or run them without opening the app.

MARK DONE FROM THE NOTIFICATION
- When a chore assignment push arrives, long-press it and tap "Mark done" to complete the chore right from the banner. No app launch needed.

PHOTO-PROOF CHORES
- New "Requires photo proof" toggle when creating a chore. Whoever completes it attaches a photo of the finished job before the points land.
- Admins see the photo in the chore detail and can Reject it - that re-opens the chore, takes back the points, and clears the photo for a redo.
- Proof chores show a camera icon on the admin Big Board.

GROUP GOALS: EVERYONE PITCHES IN
- New reward type alongside the whole-family pool: "Everyone pitches in" unlocks only when EVERY family member individually hits the target (e.g. everyone reaches 100 pts = movie night).
- The goal card shows who's made it and who hasn't (dimmed avatars), and it's a celebration milestone - nobody's points get spent.

SMARTER MORNING BRIEFING
- The daily briefing now reads like a human wrote it: "3 chores due - Soccer at 5:00 PM - 2 reward requests waiting - Dakoda (fire)7" instead of bare counts.

What to Test
- Say "Hey Siri, add a chore to Casalist" and confirm the chore lands in the app.
- Create a chore with photo proof ON, complete it from the kid's side, confirm the photo prompt appears and the photo shows in the chore detail. Try Reject as the admin.
- Create an "Everyone pitches in" goal from Rewards and watch the avatar row fill in as members hit the bar.
- Long-press an assignment notification and use Mark done.
