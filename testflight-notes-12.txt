What's Fixed in 3 (12)

POINTS ARE BACK
- Build 11's new seasons feature accidentally showed everyone's leaderboard score as 0. Your points were never lost -- this was a display bug in how the season score was calculated.
- This build restores everyone's points on the leaderboard, levels, and profile.
- You only need ONE person in the family to update and open the app -- it repairs the whole household automatically, so everyone's points reappear even before they update.

HOW SEASONS WORK NOW
- Season 1 counts all the points you've already earned, so nothing resets on rollout.
- Future seasons start a fresh race every 60 days.
- Your lifetime levels are permanent (see them in your profile), and your spendable points wallet is never affected by a season.
