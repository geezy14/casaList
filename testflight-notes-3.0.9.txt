Casalist 3.0 (build 9)

Levels, tiers, and rewards got a big rebalance.

What's new
- Simpler tiers. Three clear tiers replace the old medal tiers:
  Sprout, Ember, and Diamond. Your avatar badge now always
  matches your actual level (no more "1st place but a bronze
  medal" mix-ups).
- Reachable levels. The climb to Legend was rebalanced - levels
  come fast early and ramp up later, and Legend is now 1,300
  points instead of 2,700 so it doesn't feel impossible.
- Rebalanced rewards. Point costs were rescaled to match
  (5 points per $1), and the redeemable catalog was repriced.
  Existing households update automatically - you can still tweak
  any value in Settings -> Game Rules.

What's fixed
- No more ghost event notifications. A reminder for an event you
  deleted or renamed will no longer keep firing.
- Sharing your profile card works again.

What to test
- Check your rank: avatar badge tier should match your level
  name (e.g. Chore Warrior = Ember).
- Confirm reward and redeem point costs look rescaled.
- Delete or rename an event, then confirm its old notification
  doesn't fire later.
- Open your profile card and tap Share.

Thanks!
