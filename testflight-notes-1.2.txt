What's New in 2.0 Build 1.2

REWARDS & LEVELS
- Kid mode now works both ways -- admins can switch a member to kid OR standard and it takes effect on their device instantly
- Level names updated: Broom Pilot, Mop Jockey, Chore Warrior, Task Slayer (levels 2-5)
- Your rank and level now show under your name on the Personal Card -- updates dynamically as you earn points
- Rank badges added under each name in the This Week's Star leaderboard
- Personal Card sections renamed: Swept the Chores, Chores & Scores, Keeping Up the Pace
- Share card fixed -- tapping Share now goes straight to the share sheet

HOME & CHORES
- Home and Maintenance tabs now show a default chore tile grid -- tap any tile to assign it to a family member and add it to their to-do
- Grocery tab has a new staple quick-add grid -- tap to add instantly, tile dims when item is already on the list
- Quick Add on the home dashboard is now a tile grid matching the same style

FAMILY LIST
- Up for Grabs section is now 2-column tiles (was a plain list)
- Points always visible on each tile
- Quick-add defaults to 5 pts

QUICK TASK BAR
- Every tab now has a Quick Task bar -- type and submit without opening the full form
- Home, My To-Do, Maintenance, and Family List all have it

BUG FIXES
- Tasks due today no longer show as overdue the moment they are created
- Reminders tile count is now per-person -- assigned reminders only show on that person's tile and only they get the push. Unassigned reminders show for everyone

What to Test
- Switch a family member between kid and standard mode and confirm it updates on their device
- Tap a default chore tile on the Home/Maintenance tab and assign it to someone
- Add a grocery staple -- confirm it dims when already on the list
- Use the Quick Task bar on each tab
- Set a reminder assigned to one person -- confirm only they see it on their tile
