Casalist 1 (build 1.2) — Critical fix + Rewards approvals on standings

CRITICAL FIX (please install before you delete and reinstall)
- Fixed a bug where deleting and reinstalling the app could wipe the
 entire family from every device. Cause: an auto-cleanup routine
 mistook the not-yet-synced CloudKit household for stray garbage and
 deleted it, propagating the delete to every family member. Disabled
 the auto-deletion entirely; duplicates (rare) are now cleaned up
 manually in Settings.

NEW
- Rewards page: pending reward requests now show as a peach badge
 directly on the requesting kid's avatar in standings. Admin taps
 the avatar to open an inline approval sheet with the requester's
 "why" note and a points stepper. Removed the tray icon from this
 page — requests live where the data lives.
- Settings -> Data -> "Reset all data" now also clears streaks and
 badges. (Was leaving them behind.)

WHAT TO TEST
- Normal use should be unchanged. The fix is defensive — it just
 stops a destructive auto-cleanup from running too eagerly.
- If anyone in the family deletes and reinstalls the app, they
 should get all the existing family data back via CloudKit sync
 (give it 1-2 minutes after first launch).
- As admin, have a kid suggest a reward. Their avatar in your
 Rewards standings should show a pending count. Tap to approve.
