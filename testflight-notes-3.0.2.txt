Casalist 3.0 (build 2)

What's new
- Pick your layout. Settings -> Appearance -> Layout now has four:
  Classic, Rings, Calm, Kanban. Switch any time.
- Pulsing profile chip on the Dashboard taps into your chore card.
- "Announce to household" toggle on calendar events. Pick a kid
  as the attendee, still ping the whole family.
- "Weekdays only" repeat option for Schedule events.
- Loose reminders (no specific person, no admin group) live in
  the Reminders tab only -- they no longer crowd anyone's To-Do.

What to test
- Settings -> Appearance -> Layout. Cycle through all four.
- Create a Schedule event, pick a kid, toggle "Announce to
  household" -- confirm everyone gets the push.
- Schedule a recurring event with "Weekdays only" -- shouldn't
  fire Saturday or Sunday.
- Cross-device sync should still be instant on every layout.
