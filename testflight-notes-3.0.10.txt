Casalist 3.0 (build 10)

Bug fixes.

What's fixed
- Share your profile card actually works now. Tapping Share opens
  a preview of your card so you see exactly what you're sending,
  then Share or Save to Photos from there. (It used to come up
  blank.)
- Given points count toward your level. Points an admin awards
  with the +/- buttons now raise your level too, not just your
  spendable balance - so a kid given points levels up like they
  should (no more "Rookie" with a high score).

What to test
- Open your profile card, tap Share, confirm the preview shows
  your card, then share or save it.
- As an admin, give a member points with the + button and confirm
  their level/tier name updates to match their total.

Thanks!
