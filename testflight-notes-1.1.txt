Casalist 1 (build 1.1) — Family rollout day

This is the first build the rest of the family will use. Welcome!

WHAT'S NEW IN 1.1
• Settings → Data → "Reset all data" (admin only). Wipes every chore,
 goal, event, streak, badge, and quick-add — points reset to 0 — but
 keeps the household and every family member intact. Use it if you
 want a clean start without re-inviting everyone.

EVERYTHING ELSE IS UNCHANGED FROM 1.0
• Kid mode (starfield) for family members marked as Kid.
• Three color themes in Settings → Appearance: Ember (default), Vivid,
 Anchor. Swap any time.
• Push notifications when you get a new task, when a reward request
 needs approval (admins), and when a family member redeems a reward.
 Weekly Sunday recap of standings.
• Goal economy: anyone can ask for a reward (label + optional "why");
 admins set the points price at approval.
• "Home" tile bundles home + maintenance with a pill toggle inside.
• Quick-add chips on Home for one-tap chore creation.
• Pull-to-refresh on every scrollable screen.

WHAT TO TEST
• Add a chore assigned to someone else. Within ~30s they should get a
 push and see it on their dashboard.
• Have a kid hit "Ask for a reward" with a note. Approve from your
 inbox with a price you set. Watch the kid's shelf update.
• Check off chores back-and-forth between phones to verify points
 sync and the WHAT'S NEW feed picks up the correct doer/time.
• Try the Reset all data button if you want to start clean before
 real use. Cannot be undone.
