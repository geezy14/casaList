What's New in 3 (17)

HOUSEHOLD BOARD (admins)
- A new admin card on the Dashboard opens a household overview: a summary band (chores today, overdue, pending approvals, points this week), today's calendar, overdue chores, this week's events, reminders, and per-member chore progress with streaks.

ANNOUNCEMENTS
- Post an announcement from Family List (megaphone). It pushes to everyone and shows as a banner on the Dashboard for 24 hours, or until you clear it.

QUICK ADD
- The + button opens the add screen directly, now with three tabs: New task, New bundle, and Reminder.
- Single tasks can now carry a bonus -- extra points awarded on completion, on top of the base points.

NAVIGATION
- Settings now slides in: swipe right from the left edge of the Dashboard (the cog is gone).
- You can swipe to exit any screen.

MORE
- My To-Do is now focused on just you.
- New Streaks & Badges screen (the coral Dashboard tile): per-member streaks and earned badges.
- Reminders now live inside My To-Do, the quick-add Reminder tab, and the Household Board.

What to Test
- Open the Household Board card and confirm the overview looks right.
- Post an announcement from Family List; confirm everyone gets a push and the Dashboard banner shows for 24h.
- Make a task with a bonus; complete it and confirm the bonus is awarded.
- Swipe right on the Dashboard to open Settings; swipe to exit other screens.
- Confirm chores, points, and syncing all still work across devices.
